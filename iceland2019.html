One Trip, Three Persepctives

In December of 2019 I spent a week in Iceland, staying in the capital city of Reykjavik. Below are 3 Google Maps that outline my trip from the flight, to all the places I visited, to a specific one day tour.

Flight path from Toronto Pearson International Airport to Kelflavík International Airport in Iceland.

Some tourist attractions and museums I visited in and around the Reykjavik area.

The route for a guided bus tour hosted by Reykjavik Sightseeing. We went to Þingvellir National Park, Strokkur (an erupting geyser), Gullfoss Waterfall and visited the towns of Flúðir and Hveragerði. At night, we saw the Northern Lights.
